Why Choose Us

Better Future

We update you with the latest Microsoft and Open Source technologies and knowledge. You will become one of the leading business professional. You will be prepared to accept challenges while reaching your goal.

Job Opportunity

You will get to know industry professionals from leading organizations and societies who are partnered with Microsoft and open source communities while making an advantage for you to face an job interview without any hesitation.

Workshops

Opportunity to participate in technological and educational workshops organized by Edvicon Int. Not only that, you will also be guided and earn the opportunity to participate in external Microsoft and non-profit affiliated workshops.

Bootcamps

We provide the technical training Microsoft related and open source programs enabling you to focus on the most important aspects of coding and various aspects immediately and apply the acquired skills to solve real-world issues.

Competitions

We organize various competitions as intra-university and inter-university enabling students to highlight their skills and learn new things on Microsoft and open source products, enabling you to be part of the history of champions.

Webinars

This is the best opportunity for students to engage in workshops/ training on Microsoft and open source products those who can not reach physically. This enables to accommodate a large number of students to be educated easily.
